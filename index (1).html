About us

Informed, engaged and empowered young citizens

Civic Voices Lab – Central Asia is a youth-led civic education initiative. We empower young people through human rights education, civic engagement, leadership development, entrepreneurship, public dialogue, and community action.

We provide free educational resources, organize workshops and debates, publish youth-written articles and essays, and create real opportunities for meaningful civic participation across the region.

Our core values

Human RightsYouth LeadershipInnovationDialogue EqualityEducationCommunity EngagementIntegrityCritical Thinking

Our mission

To create informed, engaged, and empowered young citizens who actively contribute to stronger, more inclusive communities throughout Central Asia.

Our vision

A generation of young people across Central Asia who understand their rights, lead with integrity, and turn dialogue into lasting community impact.

Share Your Voice with Central Asia

Civic Voices Lab publishes selected articles, essays, research, opinion pieces and personal stories free of charge to amplify youth voices across the region. There are no publication fees.

We accept

Human Rights Articles Research Papers Opinion Essays Community Stories Youth Initiatives Women's Empowerment Entrepreneurship Education Climate & Civic Life

Submission guidelines

  • Original work only
  • Proper citations where needed
  • Respectful, inclusive language
  • Educational or informative
  • 800–2,500 words
  • EN, UZ, RU (others welcome)
★ No publication fees

Publish Your Work for Free

Email your submission, a short biography and an optional profile photo. Our editorial team reviews every piece and helps selected authors reach readers across Central Asia.
